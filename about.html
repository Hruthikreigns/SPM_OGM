About Santhipuram Main Road Committee

Welcome to the official page of the Santhipuram Main Road Committee. Our committee was formed by the residents of Santhipuram to celebrate the grand festival of Vinayaka Chavithi every year with unity, tradition, and devotion.

Our Purpose

Since 2014, we have organized community-wide Ganesh Chaturthi celebrations that include:

  • Installation of Lord Vinayaka idol
  • Pooja ceremonies and rituals
  • Cultural programs and events
  • Prasadam distribution
  • Community engagement and volunteer work

Our Vision

To keep the tradition of Vinayaka Chavithi alive in our community, foster unity among residents, and provide a platform for all ages to participate in meaningful celebrations.
