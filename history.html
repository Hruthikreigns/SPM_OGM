Our History

The Santhipuram Main Road Committee began its journey in 2014, bringing the community together through the celebration of Vinayaka Chavithi and many cultural activities.

Since the beginning, the team has organized vibrant cultural programs including traditional dance, devotional poojas, grand fireworks, and annadanam (free meals) throughout the festival. Each year features a prestigious band set and the most awaited grand laddu auction that reflects the pride and unity of Santhipuram.

Founded by:

Appu, Harsha, Bharath G, Sathish, Vinay, Sunil, Ganga, Vicky, Rajesh, Raju, Dhanush

These founders laid the strong roots for unity and celebration that continues every year.
